I seem to have been rotting for a long time; perhaps I died when I was 14, and what followed was only more intense and absurd torture inflicted on a dead corpse. This may be why I haven't felt anything for the past few years.
I deeply admire those websites with strong colors and distinctive styles... I hope that, in the future, I might recover the feelings that were gradually torn away from me by endless strain. (I'm speaking now in a way that would have felt absurd to my younger self... I remember that all my childhood drawings were in black and white, and I was obsessed with textures and patterns, even seeing them as the meaning of life—especially under intense negative emotions. Being autistic, this happened to me quite often as a child.)
